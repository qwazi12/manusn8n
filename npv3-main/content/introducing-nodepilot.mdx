We're beyond excited to introduce **NodePilot** — your personal AI workflow architect designed to turn ideas into clean, working n8n automations in seconds.

## Why NodePilot Exists

Automation today is messy, manual, and way too technical for most people.

- Too many nodes.
- Too much trial & error.
- Too little guidance.

NodePilot flips that. You tell it what you want — it handles the hard part.

## Our Mission

1. **Make Automation Easy for Everyone** — Describe what you want in natural language, get back optimized n8n JSON ready to run.
2. **Teach Along the Way** — Every automation comes with clear, step-by-step instructions so you understand *why* it works.
3. **Help You Build Faster** — Refine your automation in real-time AI chat until it's exactly what you need.

## What NodePilot Gives You

- **AI-Generated n8n Workflows** — Ideas in → Optimized JSON out
- **Step-By-Step Explanations** — Understand every node and connection
- **Real-Time Chat Refinement** — Tweak workflows like you're chatting with an automation expert
- **Support for Files & Screenshots** — Upload context to guide the AI
- **Workflow History & Storage** — Free users get 2-4 weeks, Pro users get 1-3 months saved history
